**Legal from Day One** is more than just compliance — it’s about treating law as part of the product, not an obstacle.

When building in Web3 or AI, it’s very easy to say: let’s build first, and bring in the lawyers later.

We want to flip that logic.

In this track, we’re looking for projects that:
– have regulatory compliance built in from the very first commit,
– solve real legal problems for users,
– or use technology to simplify the law and increase trust.

It’s not about knowing every regulation.
It’s about asking the right questions:
👉 who is responsible?
👉 where is the data, and who controls it?
👉 is the user protected?

The best projects in this category won’t just be innovative — they’ll be ready for the real world.

And that’s often what determines whether a project lasts longer than a demo.
