## **Prize Pool 💰**

- **1st Place:** 5,000 PLN
- **2nd Place:** 3,000 PLN
- **3rd Place:** 2,000 PLN

## **Challenge Overview**

This is a broad AI-focused challenge open to all kinds of AI-powered solutions. While any creative and valuable AI use case is welcome, submissions that align closely with **TAURON’s business areas, customer needs, and long-term strategy** are especially encouraged.

We are looking for innovative ideas that can bring real value to TAURON customers, improve operational efficiency, or support the [company’s strategic goals](https://raport.tauron.pl/strategia-i-perspektywy/strategia-grupy-tauron-do-2030-r-cele-i-priorytety/) 🌍

## **Suggested Areas of Focus**

You are free to propose any AI solution, but here are some directions that are especially welcome:

### **1. Data Analysis Projects 📊**

Solutions that analyze and transform data into actionable insights, especially if they relate to:

- electricity consumption
- energy usage patterns
- customer behavior
- operational efficiency
- forecasting and optimization

### **2. Customer Service Innovation 💬**

AI projects that improve the customer experience in TAURON-related services, for example:

- smarter customer support tools
- AI chatbots
- personalized recommendations
- automation of repetitive customer service tasks
- better digital assistance for users

### **3. Energy Consumption Tools 🔌**

Ideas related to helping customers understand and optimize their electricity usage, such as:

- energy consumption calculators
- smart consumption estimates
- dynamic tariff guidance
- energy-saving recommendations
- household or business usage profiling

### **4. Mobile App Enhancements 📱**

TAURON already has a mobile app — but we are especially interested in ideas for new AI-powered features that would make it much more useful for users.

Examples could include:

- personalized energy insights
- proactive alerts and recommendations
- consumption prediction
- intelligent assistant features
- improved user support and navigation

### **5. Advanced Chatbot Solutions 🤝**

A classic chatbot is welcome, but we are especially excited by more advanced and creative approaches, such as:

- context-aware customer support
- AI assistants that understand user intent more deeply
- multilingual or locally adapted communication
- a chatbot that can respond in **Silesian dialect** for a unique and engaging user experience 🗣️

## **What We Value ⭐**

Submissions will be strongest if they demonstrate:

- practical business value
- clear use of AI
- relevance to TAURON’s ecosystem
- user impact and usability
- originality and creativity
- feasibility of implementation

## **Final Note**

We are open to all AI ideas, but the most impressive submissions will be those that connect innovation with TAURON’s mission, customers, and energy-related services ⚡
