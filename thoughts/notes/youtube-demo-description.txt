Title: Mój Tauron AI — AI Energy Advisor for TAURON (ETH Silesia 2026)

Description:

Mój Tauron AI — a working prototype of the 24/7 virtual contact center that TAURON Polska Energia's own 2030 strategy calls for. Built at the ETH Silesia 2026 hackathon.

January 2026. Poland's energy price freeze ends. Bills jump overnight, call centers queue for hours. Mój Tauron AI replaces the form, the hold music, and the queue — with a single conversation.

Two demos in one video:
— Customer view: Anna Kowalska asks why her bill spiked, verifies by SMS, sees a 36-month chart, compares tariffs, signs a new contract with mObywatel. No form. No phone call. No redirect.
— Backoffice view: a TAURON operator corrects the agent with a live FAQ (vector-embedded on save) and builds a new widget from one plain-language brief. No code, no release cycle.

── Stack ──

Next.js 16 (App Router) · LangGraph · LiteLLM · Drizzle ORM + Postgres · Auth.js v5 (JWT) · ElevenLabs TTS · BulkGate SMS · Railway · TypeScript strict · pnpm workspaces · LangSmith observability

── What makes this more than a Q&A bot ──

— Widgets, not walls of text. Structured JSON from the LLM renders as charts, comparators, and a contract-signing widget with mObywatel built in.
— Compliance as architecture. Four file-suffix rules (*.server.ts, *.action.ts, *.shared.ts, *.client.ts) make secrets physically incapable of reaching the client bundle. SMS OTP is a hard gate before any personal data is touched. The LLM is boxed into a typed response enum — prompt injection cannot unlock tools.
— Polish by default, with an optional Silesian dialect toggle.
